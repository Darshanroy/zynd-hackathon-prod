# Sample Questions for Each Option

## üí∞ Check Benefits

When you select "Check Benefits", you'll be asked:

1. **What is your age?**
   - Example: 35
   - Why: Age-specific schemes like senior citizen benefits, youth programs

2. **What is your annual household income? (in ‚Çπ)**
   - Example: 200000
   - Why: Most schemes have income thresholds (Below Poverty Line, Low Income Group)

3. **Which state do you live in?**
   - Dropdown: Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka, etc.
   - Why: Many benefits are state-specific

4. **What is your employment status?**
   - Options: Employed (Full-time), Unemployed, Farmer, Self-Employed, etc.
   - Why: Employment-based schemes (MGNREGA, PM-SVANidhi, PM-KISAN)

5. **How many members are in your family?**
   - Example: 4
   - Why: Family size affects eligibility (Ayushman Bharat, ration schemes)

6. **What is your highest education level?** (Optional)
   - Options: No Formal Education, Primary, Secondary, Graduate, etc.
   - Why: Education scholarships and skill development programs

7. **Social Category** (Optional)
   - Options: General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, Prefer not to say
   - Why: Category-based reservations and special provisions

## üìú Explain Policy

When you select "Explain Policy", you'll be asked:

1. **Which policy or scheme do you want to understand?**
   - Example: PM-KISAN, MGNREGA, Ayushman Bharat, National Pension Scheme
   - Why: Identifies the policy to explain

2. **What specifically do you want to know?**
   - Options:
     - General overview of the policy
     - Who is eligible?
     - What benefits does it provide?
     - What are the obligations or requirements?
     - What are the risks or unclear areas?
     - Everything about this policy
   - Why: Focuses the explanation on what you need

3. **Any specific questions about this policy?** (Optional)
   - Example: "Does this apply to my state? Can I apply online?"
   - Why: Addresses your specific concerns

## üìù Help Fill Application

When you select "Help Fill Application", you'll be asked:

1. **Which scheme do you want to apply for?**
   - Example: PM-KISAN, Ayushman Bharat, PM-SVANidhi
   - Why: Identifies the application to help with

2. **Which documents do you already have?** (Optional, Multiple Selection)
   - Options:
     - Aadhaar Card
     - PAN Card
     - Income Certificate
     - Caste Certificate
     - Residence Proof
     - Bank Account Passbook
     - Land Ownership Documents
     - Ration Card
     - Voter ID
     - None of the above
   - Why: Determines what documents you still need

3. **What stage are you at?**
   - Options:
     - Just starting - need complete guidance
     - Gathering documents
     - Ready to fill application
     - Application submitted - tracking status
     - Application rejected - need appeal help
   - Why: Provides stage-appropriate guidance

4. **What specific help do you need?** (Optional)
   - Example: "I don't understand what 'land ownership certificate' means"
   - Why: Addresses specific challenges

## Custom Query Examples

If your question doesn't fit the 4 main options, use the custom query box:

- "What schemes are available for farmers in Karnataka?"
- "I am 65 years old, disabled, and living alone. What benefits can I get?"
- "How do I get an income certificate in Tamil Nadu?"
- "My PM-KISAN application was rejected. What should I do?"
- "What is the difference between Ayushman Bharat and state health insurance?"

The system will automatically route these to the appropriate agent!
